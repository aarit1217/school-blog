Kimberly Lane Elementary School is located in Plymouth,Minnesota on Old Rockford Rd. It is a very good Elementary School for grades K-5 and there is a lot of good reasons you should go there.

Gym

One of the most popular parts of our school.

The Gym is an important part of our school. Not only do calsses get to exercise and do Physical Activities with ou amazing P.E. teacher Mr. Clifton.But we also have alot of important gatherings there. Like at the beginning of the year, where 5th Graders have to take a pledge, and sing a song to introduce the Kindergarteners. We throw on alot of shows there. Like our Magic Show.
